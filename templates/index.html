FloodNet - IoT-Based Flood Monitoring System

The Cause

Flooding in India is primarily caused by overflowing dam waters due to inefficient monitoring systems, heavy rainfall from climate change, and communication delays in issuing alerts. Traditional methods are slow, error-prone, and lack real-time capabilities, leading to significant damage to crops, infrastructure, and loss of lives annually. Improved systems are critical to addressing these issues effectively.

Our Aim

Our aim is to develop FloodNet, a reliable IoT-based system for real-time dam water level monitoring and flood alerts. By integrating renewable energy, dual communication methods, and precise sensor technology, we strive to minimize flood risks, safeguard lives, and promote eco-friendly solutions for sustainable disaster management.

Technologies Used

  • Sensors: Magnetic float sensors for water level detection.
  • Microcontroller: Arduino UNO for control and processing.
  • Power Sources: Solar panels, battery and AC Mains for power
  • Notification System: IoT Module, Cloud Based
